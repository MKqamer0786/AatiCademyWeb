The most common questions parents ask when choosing an online tutor for their daughter — answered honestly.

01Getting Started

AatiCademy is a premium online tutoring platform exclusively for young girls in grades 1–12. We provide one-on-one live sessions with expert female educators in a safe, Islamic-values-based environment. We serve families in the USA, Canada, UK, UAE, India and beyond.

The free demo is a full 45–60 minute session — not a sales call. We match your daughter with a tutor based on her grade and subject. The tutor assesses her level and gives a clear picture of how sessions would proceed. No obligation to continue.

We teach students from Grade 1 through Grade 12 (ages approx 6–18), across all major curricula including IGCSE, IB, CBSE, and ICSE.

02Safety & Islamic Values

Yes — 100% of our tutors are female. This is a founding principle, not an afterthought.

All tutors are Muslim women who understand Islamic adab. Sessions are conducted with hijab, appropriate language, and professional boundaries. No male access to any sessions or communications. Parents may observe at any time.

Absolutely — and we encourage it, especially for younger students.

03Learning & Progress

Every student begins with an initial assessment identifying her level, learning style, strengths, and gaps. A personalized plan is created and continuously adjusted.

We will reassign — no questions asked, no penalty. The right fit matters more than anything.

04Scheduling & Cost

Highly flexible. Mornings, afternoons, evenings, or weekends — any timezone. You and your tutor agree on a recurring schedule.

Pricing is personalised based on grade, subject, and frequency — transparent, no hidden fees. Contact us for an accurate quote.
